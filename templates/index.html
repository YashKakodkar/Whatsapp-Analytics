To find your WhatsApp chat analytics:

1. Open WhatsApp on your phone

2. Open the chat you want to get its analytics of

3. Tap on the Name of the chat and scroll down to tap on Export Chat

4. Select Without Media

5. Select your method of sharing to yourself via AirDrop, Email, Dropbox or any other service

6. Download the shared zip file on your computer and unzip it

7. Upload the file down below
